About

Project Life

Project Life is not just a brand — it's a lifelong journey of self-growth, experiments, learnings, and transformations. The core belief behind it is that life itself is a project, and every person is a work-in-progress.

Through real-life challenges, personal failures, and continuous self-improvement efforts, I aim to research, implement, document, and share the impact of what works — and what doesn't.
